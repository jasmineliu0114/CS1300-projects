Quest Water Dispenser: How has COVID-19 impacted design?

A few months ago, the Data Science Institute at Brown University replaced their old water dispenser with a new Quest COVID-19 model. This new model introduces hovering to activate sensors, removing the need for users to touch the machine. However, this begs the questions: what sorts of trade-offs does this new feature introduce in the design of a water dispenser?
